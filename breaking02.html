Torture testimonies for minors during their arrest

The Commission of Detainees and Ex-Prisoners Affairs documented arrest certificates for children who were severely beaten by the occupation police during their arrest, including the child Muhammad Alaa Esmat Obaid (15 years), Al-Isawiya district of Jerusalem, who at the moment of his arrest at 1:30 p.m. was beaten by the Israeli police and tied with tight plastic chains After that, they took him to the Al-Buraq Wall area for four continuous hours, and from there he was transferred to the Al-Qashleh Police, and then to Al-Maskobiya Prison, where he remained for 13 days, after which he was transferred to Al-Damoon Prison, where he is staying.

As for the case of the prisoner, the child Montaser Eid Sebta (18 years), from the town of Al-Isawiya, Jerusalem district, was arrested from the courtyards of the Al-Aqsa Mosque at 1:30 p.m. after he was brutally attacked by the Israeli police, who then took him to the Qishla police, to interrogate him while he was shackled with plastic handcuffs and feet.
